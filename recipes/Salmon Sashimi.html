Salmon Sashimi

Description

Freshly cut Salmon fillet slices. Each is perfectly cut to be of uniform size. Salmon with higher fat content is typically chosen for Sashimi.

Ingredients

  • Salmon Filet
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Lemon
  • Greens for garnish

Steps

  1. Get a salmon filet and gut into three smaller even filets
  2. Put filets on a rack and rub with plenty of salt and sugar on all sides
  3. Put rack in fridge for 45 minutes
  4. Prepare two bowls of water. Dip filets in the first bowl then throughly clean them in the second
  5. Rub filets dry with a towel
  6. Cut the filets into small even rectangular slices
  7. Serve with soy sauce, lemon, and wasabi. Optionally greens for garnish
